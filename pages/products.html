Scrabbit

Scrabbit is an augmented reality scrapbook platform that allows users to upload and share their scrapbooks with each other, creating a unique and interactive experience for everyone. Users can edit, save, add text and draw on their scrapbooks, and easily upload photos and mementos to personalize their scrapbooks. Once their scrapbook is complete, users can upload the scrapbook to their profile, where their friends and family can comment and like each other's scrapbooks via the app, allowing them to experience their memories together in a way that is truly unique. Users also have the functionality to privately message their friends and discover new scrapbooks around them by location or by discovering them through their feeds.
